About Us

A reviewing Hostel Platform

As a fresher, when you join a college/university, a very big challenge is to find accommodations near your college. A large number of students have to go through the struggle of looking for hostels/rooms through references from different sources. Thus, to help the students in finding the adequate accommodation, a platform like “Find me Hostel” is needed.

Objectives

  • To help students find accommodation according to their preferences
  • To provide authentic reviews of the properties Help landlords lease their rooms easily
  • To create a catalog of best room prices for students To get all relevant information regarding the hostels like contact details, etc.
  • Eliminate middleman in the renting process
